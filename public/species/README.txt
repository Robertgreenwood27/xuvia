XUVIA Field Guide photos
========================

Drop one photo per species into this folder, named after the species slug.
Use .jpg or .png (webp shows on the site but not on share cards).
Roughly square or portrait images look best. Keep them under ~1 MB.

Current slugs:

  greenbottle-blue-tarantula.jpg
  gooty-sapphire-ornamental.jpg
  socotra-island-blue-baboon.jpg
  togo-starburst-baboon.jpg
  orange-baboon-tarantula.jpg

The photo automatically appears in three places:
  1. The Field Guide index card (/species)
  2. The species page, as a framed specimen plate
  3. The share card when the species page is linked anywhere

New species: the slug comes from lib/species.js.
